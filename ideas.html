What founders bring

Problem + Stage: Clear startup problem and current stage.

Sprint Priorities: Near-term priorities for the next sprint.

Roles Needed: Product, growth, design, or operations roles.

Communication: Consistent updates with the team each week.

What students commit to

Weekly Availability: Consistent time each week.

Task Ownership: Clear ownership of tasks and outcomes.

Team Communication: Active communication with founders and teammates.

Real Delivery: Real startup output, not simulation work.
