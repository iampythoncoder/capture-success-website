How It Works

Capture Success runs on a simple operating model: clear startup scope, matched contributors, weekly delivery, and transparent ownership records. Project BEACON, led by Saatvik Santosh and Ketav Karthikeyan, won $1,000 using this exact model and set the performance bar for future teams.

What founders bring

  • Clear startup problem and current stage
  • Near-term priorities for the next sprint window
  • Roles needed across product, growth, or operations
  • Consistent communication with the contributor team

What students commit to

  • Consistent weekly availability
  • Ownership of specific tasks and measurable outcomes
  • Active communication with founders and teammates
  • Execution in real startup conditions, not simulations
